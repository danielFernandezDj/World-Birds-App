Canary has multiple meanings.

The canary is a small songbird native to the Canary Islands, Madeira, and the Azores. The wild canary is usually greenish-brown with streaks, but domestic canaries have been selectively bred to come in many colors, including yellow, orange, brown, black, white, and red.
